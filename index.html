About Me
I am a third year undergraduate student at Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur. I like to solve problems through code and that is what drives me. My interests include Programming and Web Development but recently I have been exploring App Development as well.

Even though I'm not majoring in Computer Science, I enjoy working with software. I've always been intriguied by computers. Back when we were taught Java in school in grade 9th, I made a few games like TicTacToe and MovieGusser in Java. Fun Fact -- My first programming language was LOGO (:P)

I am also a UI/UX enthusiast. I love to design good interfaces and bring them to reality. I actually started with graphic designing but that soon turned into designing good UIs. I haven't had much experience in UI design though but it's just one thing which I like doing. Apart from these, I like to listen to music and play Table Tennis in my free time.
Projects
Co-bot : A COVID 19 chatbot
Developed a Python chat-bot which gave facts, statistics and answered other queries related to COVID-19. This project was done as a part of Techweek conducted by the SnT Council IITK during freshman year. The web app is developed using Flask, Bootstrap and some HTML CSS. It is deployed at Heroku.

Music Player in JAVA
